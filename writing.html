No Smoking
Logline: A desperate man's attempt at a fresh start ignites into violent and surreal encounters, forcing him to confront his past as his choices lead him down a path of fiery consequences and eternal damnation.
Genre: [Placeholder Genre] | Date: (2024)
Summary: The story centers on Creed Isaacs, a man in his fifties attempting to rebuild his life after a long period of homelessness and addiction. Creed secures a job at a Fuel City gas station owned by Garfield Gallard. This new beginning is fraught with challenges. Creed faces disdain from his younger coworker, Steven McGee, who resents Creed's presence. Despite Creed's efforts, he is caught smoking on the job, a direct violation of Fuel City's policy due to the old gas pumps, and is subsequently terminated by Gallard. Following his firing, Creed steals Steven's car from the Fuel City parking lot. In a state of desperation, Creed succumbs to his past and relapses with the help of an acquaintance, Siggy Meddlers.
